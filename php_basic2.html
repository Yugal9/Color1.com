Variables and Data Types

In this script, we define variables and display their values.

Program


$name = "John";
$age = 30;
$isStudent = true;
echo "Name: " . $name . "< br>";
echo "Age: " . $age . "< br>";
echo "Is student? " . ($isStudent ? "Yes" : "No") . "< br>";
?>

OUTPUT


Name: John
Age: 30
Is student? Yes
